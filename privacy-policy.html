Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Welcome to Count Up Kids. We are committed to protecting children's privacy and complying with Google Play Families Policy Requirements.

1. Information We Collect

Count Up Kids does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

  • No names
  • No email addresses
  • No phone numbers
  • No location data
  • No device identifiers

2. Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for children and complies with:

  • Google Play Families Policy
  • COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)

Since we do not collect personal data, children can safely use the app without submitting any personal information.

3. Third-Party Services

Count Up Kids does not use:

  • Third-party analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • External SDKs that collect user data

4. Internet Connection

The app does not require an internet connection to function.

5. Local Storage

App settings such as voice and background music preferences are stored locally on the user's device and are never transmitted externally.

6. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy if necessary. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
