Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Thank you for using Count Up Kids. We are committed to protecting children's privacy and complying with Google Play's Families Policy Requirements.

1. Information We Collect

Count Up Kids does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

  • No names
  • No email addresses
  • No phone numbers
  • No location data
  • No device identifiers

2. Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for children. We comply with:

  • Google Play Families Policy
  • COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)

Since we do not collect any personal data, children can use the app safely without providing any personal information.

3. Third-Party Services

Count Up Kids does not use:

  • Third-party analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • External SDKs that collect user data

4. Internet Connection

The app does not require an internet connection to function.

5. In-App Settings

The app includes optional settings such as voice and background music. These settings are stored locally on the device and are not transmitted externally.

6. Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is no risk of data exposure through our application.

7. Changes to This Policy
